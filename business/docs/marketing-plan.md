# Marketing Plan

## Channels

### 1. X/Twitter (Primary)
- Post demos, tips, before/after
- Engage in OpenClaw community threads
- DM potential leads
- **Goal:** 5 posts/week, 20+ DMs/week

### 2. Reddit
- Subreddits: r/LocalLLaMA, r/AiForSmallBusiness, r/AgentsOfAI
- Share useful info, not just spam
- **Goal:** 3 posts/week

### 3. Indie Hacker Forums
- Product Hunt launch
- Hacker News discussions
- **Goal:** 1 launch post

### 4. YouTube
- Tutorial: "How to set up OpenClaw (non-technical)"
- Mention our service at end
- **Goal:** 1 video/month

### 5. Content Marketing
- Blog posts on common setups
- "OpenClaw vs ChatGPT" comparison
- **Goal:** 2 posts/month
