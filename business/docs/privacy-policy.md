# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Data We Collect
- **Personal Info:** Name, email, phone (provided by you)
- **Technical Info:** VPS details, configuration preferences

## How We Use Data
- To provide setup and hosting services
- To communicate with you about your service
- To process payments

## Data We DON'T Collect
- Your LLM API keys (you provide directly to your instance)
- Your messages or AI conversations
- Access to your OpenClaw instance (unless explicitly granted for support)

## Self-Hosted = Your Data is Yours
OpenClaw runs on your VPS. We never have access to:
- Your messages
- Your calendar/email
- Your files or data

## Payments
- Payment processing via Stripe (we never see your card details)
- Invoice data stored securely
